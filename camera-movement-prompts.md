# Hollywood Camera Movement Prompts for Seedance 2

## 1. Slow Push-In

A lonely detective stands beneath a streetlight in the rain.

The camera slowly pushes in from a wide shot to an intimate close-up.

Neon reflections shimmer on the wet pavement.

Cinematic thriller atmosphere, shallow depth of field.

---

## 2. Dramatic Pull-Out

A warrior kneels beside a fallen king.

The camera slowly pulls backward revealing the massive battlefield around them.

Smoke drifts through the frame.

Epic historical film style.

---

## 3. Orbit Shot

A powerful sorceress stands in the center of an ancient temple.

The camera performs a smooth 360-degree orbit around the character.

Floating magical particles illuminate the scene.

Fantasy blockbuster cinematography.

---

## 4. Crane Rise Reveal

An explorer discovers a hidden city deep within the jungle.

The camera rises vertically above the character.

Ancient ruins gradually emerge from the forest canopy.

Adventure film aesthetics.

---

## 5. Crane Down Entrance

A luxury sports car arrives at a private mansion.

The camera begins high above before descending toward the vehicle.

Golden sunset lighting.

Premium commercial style.

---

## 6. Tracking Shot

A secret agent walks through a crowded train station.

The camera tracks smoothly alongside.

Background crowds move naturally.

Spy thriller cinematography.

---

## 7. Follow Shot

A samurai runs through a bamboo forest.

The camera follows directly behind.

Branches move dynamically through the frame.

Epic action movie atmosphere.

---

## 8. Dolly-In Suspense

A scientist discovers an unknown lifeform.

The camera slowly dollies toward the creature.

Laboratory lights flicker.

Suspenseful science-fiction mood.

---

## 9. Dolly-Out Isolation

A young woman stands alone in an empty city square.

The camera gradually pulls away.

The environment feels increasingly vast and lonely.

Emotional cinematic storytelling.

---

## 10. FPV Drone Dive

A futuristic city stretches beneath the clouds.

The camera dives rapidly between skyscrapers.

Dynamic motion and realistic speed.

High-budget sci-fi sequence.

---

## 11. Drone Reveal

A giant medieval castle emerges from dense morning fog.

The camera flies forward and rises above the landscape.

Golden sunlight illuminates the towers.

Epic fantasy opening shot.

---

## 12. Whip Pan Transition

A bounty hunter spots his target.

The camera executes a fast whip pan.

The target instantly appears across the street.

Action-thriller editing style.

---

## 13. Circular Tracking Shot

Two rivals confront each other before a duel.

The camera circles around both characters.

Tension builds with every rotation.

Cinematic drama.

---

## 14. Low-Angle Push-In

A superhero stands atop a skyscraper.

The camera slowly pushes forward from a low angle.

Powerful heroic composition.

Hollywood blockbuster style.

---

## 15. High-Angle Pull-Out

An exhausted climber reaches a mountain summit.

The camera pulls upward and backward.

The scale of the landscape is revealed.

Inspirational adventure film.

---

## 16. Steadicam Walkthrough

A mafia boss enters a luxury casino.

The camera follows continuously through multiple rooms.

Guests and staff naturally react.

One-take cinematic realism.

---

## 17. Side Tracking Commercial

A Formula One car speeds along a race track.

The camera tracks perfectly beside the vehicle.

Motion blur and speed lines.

Premium automotive commercial.

---

## 18. Overhead Rotation

A warrior lies injured on a battlefield.

The camera rotates overhead.

Smoke and chaos surround the scene.

Dramatic war-film storytelling.

---

## 19. Push-In to Extreme Close-Up

A poker player studies his opponent.

The camera slowly pushes toward the eyes.

Background sounds fade.

High tension cinematic moment.

---

## 20. Epic Ending Crane Shot

A hero stands on a cliff overlooking the kingdom.

The camera slowly rises and pulls away.

The landscape expands endlessly into the distance.

Hollywood cinematic finale.
