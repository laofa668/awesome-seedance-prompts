# 20 Director-Level Seedance 2 Cinematic Prompts

## 1. One-Take Gangster Entrance

A powerful crime boss exits a black vintage car and enters a luxury hotel.

Continuous one-take tracking shot following behind the character.

35mm lens, shallow depth of field, warm tungsten lobby lighting.

Background guests naturally move aside as he walks.

Martin Scorsese style cinematic realism.

---

## 2. Warrior Before Battle

A medieval commander prepares for battle at sunrise.

Slow push-in camera movement from wide shot to close-up.

Golden rim light illuminates armor edges.

Wind moves banners in the background.

Epic historical drama cinematography.

---

## 3. Space Station Window

An astronaut stares out of a giant observation window overlooking Earth.

Slow camera orbit around the subject.

Soft blue practical lighting inside the station.

Realistic reflections on glass surfaces.

Interstellar inspired cinematic atmosphere.

---

## 4. Desert Caravan

A massive caravan travels across endless desert dunes.

Drone shot begins high above before descending.

Long shadows stretch across the sand.

Natural heat distortion visible in the distance.

Large-scale cinematic worldbuilding.

---

## 5. Cyberpunk Alley

A hacker stands beneath flickering neon signs.

Handheld camera slowly approaches the subject.

Neon reflections on wet concrete.

Volumetric fog and atmospheric particles.

Blade Runner inspired visual storytelling.

---

## 6. Viking Throne Room

A Viking king sits on an ancient throne.

Slow dolly movement through torch-lit hall.

Firelight flickers across carved wooden walls.

Heavy cinematic contrast and shadows.

Historical fantasy epic.

## 7. Rooftop Showdown
